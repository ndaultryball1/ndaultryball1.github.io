Following the motto "You don't understand it until you can explain it", this blog is intended to host explanations of concepts in mathematics, physics or computer science that I find interesting, and would like to understand better. The intended audience is mainly myself (and maybe anyone that wants to give me a job/PhD position in future, shhh!).

I have a background in physics, and currently work in finance in London. My long term goal is to return to university and become a mathematician, and so I am currently trying to retrain myself and pick up an undergraduate education in my spare time. I'm currently working on real and functional analysis, with a view to PDEs. I'm also interested in all areas of mathematical physics and finance, and through the necessity to learn to code at work, programming languages, theoretical computer science and category theory.
